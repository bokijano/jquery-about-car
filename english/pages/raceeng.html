We all know that Formula1 is extremely fast, but the fact that we always look at other F1 cars prevents us from having a real idea about it. Even those who had the opportunity to see F1 live were deprived of a concrete comparison with a sports car, for example

Formula 1, also known as the F1, is officially called the FIA Formula One World Championship, is the highest-ranked single-seat automotive champion approved by the International Automotive Federation. The name "Formula" refers to a set of rules that competitors must adhere to. The Formula 1 season consists of a series of races known as the Grand Prix, which are held on specially built tracks or public roads. The results of each race are combined with the scoring composition that determines the two annual World Championships, one for drivers and another for the constructors. Drivers, constructors, teams, paths, judges and organizers must be the super license holders, most of the class of racing licenses issued by the International Automotive Federation.
