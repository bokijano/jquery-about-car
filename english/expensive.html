Ten most expensive cars on the world

  • Maybach Exelero - $ 8 million

    • The most expensive car in the world. The concept of German luxury is not for a serial car production. It's a prototype that is produced in just one copy. It was produced and displayed in 2005 at Tempodrom in Berlin. Coupé two-seater is located on special Fulda tires. As an "actor" he appears in a video of Jay-Z in the song "Lost One".

  • Koenigsegg CCXR Trevita - $ 4,85 million

    • The most expensive car produced in recent years. As a special edition of the Swedish hyper-car maker, CCXR Trevitin car body is made of carbon fiber, with a diamond finish. The car is so fast that you would not notice it when it went next to you on the highway.

  • Lamborghini Sesto Elemento - $ 2,2 million

    • Sesto Elemento has not even taken the road, and all twenty copies have been sold. It is estimated that the 0-100 sprint will arrive in 2.5 seconds. It has 570 hp. It is interesting that it is not everywhere legal for public traffic.

  • Pagani Zonda Cinque Roadster – $ 1,85 million

    • he Italian manufacturer of exclusive exotic, Pagani, also issued a special edition of the roadster named Cinque. It features a body of special carbon fiber, carbo-titanium. It contains titanium to increase resistance and stiffness, and reduce mass. Only five copies of this car have been produced, and this is the latest serial version of Zonda.

  • Aston Martin One-77- $ 1,85 million

    • The British supercar manufacturer has produced only 77 copies of this model. The bodywork is made of aluminum, manually. The maximum speed is 355 km / h, and the sprint is up to 100 km / h in 3.4 seconds. Only lucky people could see this car in action.

  • Maybach Landaulet - $ 1,4 million

    • A luxury luxury car. It's the most expensive limo with four doors. It is equipped with various hedonistic components, and the last part could be called a luxury apartment, which can be physically separated from the driver with whom it communicates via the intercom.

  • Ferrari LaFerrari - $ 1,3 million

    • The strongest Ferrari has a 949 hp and hybrid drive system with KERS. It is estimated that it can reach 370 km / h. Interestingly, this Ferrari, besides being the most powerful Ferrari ever produced, consumes much less fuel than its weaker "brothers". A series of 499 copies was launched.

  • McLaren P1 - $ 1,15 million

    • Although is "weaker" than the V12 LaFerrari, the hybrid power of McLaren P1 is for the respect. Even 903 hp are generated from the 3.8-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, which, with the assistance of an electric unit, can reach a speed of 350 km / h.

  • Porsche 918 Spyder - $ 845.000

    • As a German driver's perfection, it's terribly fast, but it can be classified into a group of cars called "eco friendly". It consumes very little, with an incredibly low CO2 emission for a car with such performance. The maximum speed is clean at a power of 350 km / h, and the sprint to "hundred" takes place in 2.8 seconds.

  • Leblanc Mirabeau - $ 765.000

    • Although the cheapest on this list, Leblanc Mirabeau is a serious supercar. It uses a 4.7-liter V8 with 700 hp and reaches a speed of 370 km / h. This is a real legal runner, a public transport car.
