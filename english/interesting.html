10 interesting facts about cars

Traffic is something that most of the planet's inhabitants meet everyday.

Car driving is a daily necessity, and the number of hours spent daily is not a negligible part of life.

We have highlighted a few interesting things about cars.

  • Who made the first car?

    The first car was made by Karl Benz in 1886. His motorcycle trim was successfully moving independently using a gasoline engine with internal combustion. The first cars were hand-made and without any harmonized technical characteristics. The mass production of cars and the revolution in the automotive industry is thanks to Henry Ford.

  • How many cars are currently driving in the world?

    Today, with relative precision, it is possible to say that about one billion cars are used on roads around theworld. The number of cars being produced every day is about 165,000 when it multiplies with the average age of the car, it gives a clear estimate of how powerful and important the branch of the industry is.

  • Since when is the seat belt used?

    One of the interesting things about cars is certainly the data from when the seat belt is being used. The first attempts at standard seat belts have been linked to American manufacturers Nesh and Ford in 1955. However, in 1959, the Swedish designer Nils Ivar Bolin invented the safety belt that provides effective protection because it has a three-point support in working in the Volvo car factory.

    Interestingly, Volvo has given this patent free to all car manufacturers for the sake of greater safety and life-saving.

  • When did the first radio in the car appear?

    The American manufacturer Motorola in 1930 produced the first radio receiver that allowed the reception of radio signals in the car. Its price was extremely high and amounted to about $ 130, while the comparison was made, the new Ford A car brand could be purchased at $ 540. The later development of technology has enabled the radio receiver to become an integral part of the basic equipment in cars of almost all manufacturers.>

  • Who is the biggest car manufacturer?

    In 2015, Toyota was the largest automaker and topped 10,083,783 cars. That is 150,000 vehicles more than the second-placed Volkswagen. Third place was General Motors.>

    In 2016, the Forbes list is officially announced, but Volkswagen was the leader in the first eight months of last year.

  • What is the fastest car in the world?

    At the moment, the fastest car in commercial use that can drive around the streets is the Bugatti Veyron that can develop an incredible 431 km / h.

    When the meaning of car prices most people think of the latest model of sports cars of specialized manufacturers. But, a surprise! The most expensive car that is sold is actually the old Ferrari A 1962 250 GTO, sold at auction for $ 38,650,000. This information is not surprising enough, given the fact that the number of copies made of this model is only 38.

  • How many people die in traffic accidents?

    It is estimated that around 1.25 million people per year in the world lose their lives in traffic accidents, and about 50 million people are more easily and severely injured.

    The Ginis record is currently being held by Irv Gordon from the United States, who in his Volvo 1800S has passed 4,890,993 km. The record was achieved in 2013.
